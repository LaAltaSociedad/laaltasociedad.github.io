WE ARE SOCIEDAD

We are a local clothing brand based in Marikina City that produces our own clothing line based around different themes and color palettes. We love clothes, and we know you do too! So, we are committed in bringing you only the best designs in the current trends in line with our company’s values.

Let your clothes reflect your personality!

We believe that our clothes reflect who we are. Either you are an introvert who likes to be alone, or an extrovert who likes to be around everybody, or maybe both! Either you’re a gamer who likes to play online games, or a physically active individual, or maybe all of them! To each, his own! There’s something for everybody.

We let you unleash your creativity in creating the shirt that will truly reflect who you are. These shirts you’re making, they’re not just ordinary shirts, they’re YOUR shirts! You can proudly and truly say that they’re yours and that you made them and that these reflects you.

Create, collaborate, design, and share your shirts to your friends and family!

Not so much into designing shirts?

Fear not! We serve our customers an array of design that they can choose from based on their likes and fashion styles. Choose your color, size, and order them, it’s that easy! Just like a normal clothing shop – but better of course!
